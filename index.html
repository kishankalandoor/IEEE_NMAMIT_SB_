About IEEE Mangaluru Subsection

The IEEE Mangaluru Subsection, under the IEEE Bangalore Section, is a vibrant community of engineers, researchers, and students dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. Established in [YEAR], we serve as a hub for innovation, technical learning, and professional networking in coastal Karnataka.

Our Mission

To foster technological innovation by organizing workshops, hackathons, and seminars that bridge academia and industry, while promoting IEEE's core values of excellence and collaboration.

Our Vision

To become the premier technical community in Mangaluru, empowering members to develop solutions for global challenges through cutting-edge engineering.

Steps to Join IEEE

Follow these simple steps to become an IEEE member at a 50% discount!

  1. Create an account: Click here and use your email as username.
  2. Verify your email: Enter the code sent to your email address.
  3. Login to ieee.org using your new credentials.
  4. Click "Join IEEE" and then select "Join as a Student".
  5. Fill in your details: Name, Country, Institute name ("Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Mem Inst of Tech"), address, etc.
  6. Select your status: Graduate (PG/PhD) or Undergraduate (BE/BTech), degree, and academic details.
  7. Choose your objectives for joining IEEE and continue.
  8. Societies (optional): Select any societies (extra charges) or continue.
  9. IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE): Female members can join IEEE Women in Engineering at no additional cost! WIE is the largest international professional organization dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists. Simply select the WIE membership option during your IEEE membership registration. Learn more about IEEE WIE
  10. Review your cart and continue.
  11. Payment: Choose UPI, review the total (includes 18% GST), and confirm your order.
  12. Confirmation: You’ll receive a confirmation email from IEEE.
Note: $14 for PG and $7 for UG students (plus 18% GST). Offer valid from March 01.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about IEEE membership, events, and student branch activities at NMAMIT.
Can't find what you're looking for?
Contact us for more information.

1.

How do I join IEEE as a student member?

Student membership costs just $7 per year. You can join online through the IEEE website. NMAMIT students get additional benefits including access to our local workshops, mentorship programs, and project funding opportunities.

2.

What benefits do IEEE student members receive?

IEEE student members get access to:

  • IEEE Xplore Digital Library (with limited access)
  • Discounted conference registration fees
  • Free or discounted technical workshops
  • Networking opportunities with professionals
  • Competitions and awards exclusive to student members
3.

How can I participate in IEEE events at NMAMIT?

All IEEE events are open to members and non-members, though members often get priority registration and discounts. Check our website or notice boards for upcoming events, or join our WhatsApp group for regular updates.

4.

Are there leadership opportunities in the student branch?

Yes! We elect new student leaders each academic year for positions like Chair, Vice Chair, Secretary, and various committee heads. These positions give you valuable leadership experience and look great on your resume.

5.

Can I publish my research through IEEE?

Absolutely! IEEE sponsors hundreds of conferences each year and publishes numerous journals. Student members can submit papers to appropriate conferences and may be eligible for student travel grants to present their work.

6.

Is there a deadline to join IEEE each year?

You can join IEEE at any time during the year. However, we recommend joining at the start of the academic year to maximize your benefits and participation in all annual events and competitions.

7.

How can I get involved in IEEE technical projects?

We announce project opportunities throughout the year. Members can:

  • Join existing project teams
  • Propose their own projects (funding available)
  • Participate in hackathons and design challenges
  • Collaborate with faculty on research initiatives

Attend our project fair at the start of each semester to explore opportunities.
